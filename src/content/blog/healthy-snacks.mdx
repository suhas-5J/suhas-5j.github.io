# The Myth of Healthy Snacks

In today's fast-paced world, we often reach for "healthy" bars, "natural" chips, and "sugar-free" drinks. But are they really good for us?

## The Marketing Trap

Most products labeled as "healthy" are ultra-processed foods that contain hidden sugars, artificial sweeteners, and inflammatory oils.

### What to look for:
- **Ingredient List:** If you can't pronounce it, your body might not recognize it.
- **Sugar Alcohols:** Often used in "keto" snacks but can cause digestive issues.
- **Marketing Buzzwords:** "All Natural" has no legal definition.

## The Solution: Real Food

Instead of a protein bar, try:
1. A handful of raw almonds.
2. A piece of whole fruit.
3. Greek yogurt with real berries.

Stay conscious, stay healthy.
